About Me

Hello! I'm a front-end web designer based out of Des Moines, IA. With backgrounds in both film and finance, I bring a unique blend of analytical thinking and creativity to projects. I'm always ready for new design opportunities. Whether it's a website audit or a complete overhaul, I will deliver a well-researched, well-made and impactful result.


When I'm not working on projects or learning a new web language, you can find me defying grafity as an aerialist at the Iowa Circus Academy or biking the trails to all of Des Moines's awesome craft breweries.
